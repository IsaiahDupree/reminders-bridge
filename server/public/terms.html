What NotesBridge does

NotesBridge lets an AI assistant (e.g. ChatGPT) search, read, and write the Apple Notes on your own Mac. Note operations run locally on your Mac via an agent you install; the hosted relay only forwards each request while it is in flight and does not store your note content.
