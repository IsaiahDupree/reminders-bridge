Common issues

  • "Your Mac agent is offline." Start it on your Mac with apple-notes-agent run and make sure the Mac is awake (tip: caffeinate -s while plugged in).
  • macOS says it can't control Notes. System Settings → Privacy & Security → Automation → allow your terminal app to control Notes.
  • Notes from iPhone missing. Confirm iCloud Notes sync is enabled on both the iPhone and the Mac (Settings → iCloud → Notes).
  • Connector shows unauthorized. Remove and re-add the connector in your assistant to refresh the OAuth grant.
  • Locked notes don't appear. Password-protected notes are excluded from automation by macOS — this is by design.

Uninstall / revoke access

  • Stop and unpair the Mac agent: quit the process and delete ~/.apple-notes-agent.json.
  • Remove the connector in your assistant's settings.
  • Email us to delete your account and all associated data.
